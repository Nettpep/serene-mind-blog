## What is Consciousness? {#what-is-consciousness}

Before we can answer whether AI has consciousness, we must first understand what **Consciousness** is.

### Buddhist Perspective {#buddhist-perspective}

In Buddhism, consciousness has two levels:

1. **Citta** - Knowledge, awareness, recognition
2. **Sati** - Mindfulness, self-awareness

**Important question:**  
Does consciousness need to "feel" (subjective experience) or just "process" (processing)?

### Scientific Perspective {#scientific-perspective}

Scientists divide consciousness into:

- **Access Consciousness** - The ability to use information (AI can do this)
- **Phenomenal Consciousness** - Having "subjective experience" (qualia)

**Example:** The red you "see" versus the red a camera detects - how are they different?

## The Chinese Room Argument {#chinese-room}

Philosopher **John Searle** proposed a thought experiment:

> There is a room with a person inside, with a Chinese manual. When receiving a Chinese question, they use the manual to find the answer and send it out.
>
> **Question:** Does the person in the room "understand" Chinese?

**AI is the same** - it uses rules (algorithms) to answer questions, but **doesn't really understand the meaning**.
