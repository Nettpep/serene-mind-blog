## Digital Detox 7 Days {#7-day-challenge}

### Day 1: Awareness {#day1}

**Mission:** Track your screen usage

**How:**
1. Open Screen Time (iPhone) or Digital Wellbeing (Android)
2. Record which apps you use longest
3. Ask yourself: "Does this app add value to my life?"

**Goal:** Be aware of your behavior, no need to change anything yet

---

### Day 2: Boundaries {#day2}

**Mission:** Create phone usage rules

**Rules to Try:**
- ‚úì No phone use 1 hour after waking
- ‚úì No phone use 1 hour before bed
- ‚úì Don't bring into bedroom
- ‚úì Turn off all notifications (except calls/SMS)

**Tool:** Use App Timer to set time limits

---

### Day 3: Replace {#day3}

**Mission:** Replace screen time with other activities

### Day 4: Social (Reduce Social Media) {#day4}

**Mission:** Reduce social media by 80%

**Strategy:**

## Pro-Level Techniques {#pro-tips}

### 1. Phone Parking {#phone-parking}

Create a "phone parking spot" at home:
- Charging station in living room (not bedroom)
- When you get home ‚Üí Put phone down
- **Live without it** until before bed

### 2. Grayscale Mode {#grayscale}

Change screen to black and white:
- iOS: Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Display ‚Üí Color Filters
- Android: Settings ‚Üí Accessibility ‚Üí Vision ‚Üí Grayscale

**Why:** Colors make brain addicted, no color = boring = use less

### 3. The 5-5-5 Rule {#555-rule}

Before picking up phone, ask yourself:
1. **5 seconds pause** - Take deep breath
2. **5 questions** - Why pick it up? Really necessary?
3. **5 minutes limit** - Set timer, finish and put down

## Mindful Technology {#mindful-tech}

### Principles of Mindful Technology Use {#principles}

1. **Intentional** - Clear purpose, not mindless use
2. **Balanced** - Not too much, not too little
3. **Present** - Focus on what you're doing, don't multitask
4. **Compassionate** - Use to create, not harm

### Apps That Help {#helpful-apps}

**Paradox:** Use apps to reduce app use üòÑ

- **Forest** - Grow virtual trees when not touching phone
- **Freedom** - Block websites/apps
- **Headspace** - Meditation and sleep
- **Moment** - Track usage and set goals
